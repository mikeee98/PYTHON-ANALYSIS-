Analysis on the NYPD Dataset¶

This dataset contains information about shootings that occurs in various areas of USA, The dataset indicate the affected party and their age group, race, sex and scene of crime. This dataset was collected from different law enforcement organization responsible for recording this offenses.

Features Discription¶

  • VIC SEX - Gender of the victims involed in the incident.
  • VIC RACE - The race of those victims involed.
  • PERP RACE - The race of those perpetrators.
  • PERP AGE GROUP - Ages group of perpetrators.
  • BORO - Towns that those incident occred
  • VIC AGE GROUP - Age groups of victims involed.
  • LOCATION DESC - The location the incident occured.
  • STATISTICAL MURDER_FLAG - The total cases of murder recorded.
  • Latitude - North and south region of the city.
  • Longitude - East and west region. of the city.

Analysis objective.¶

  • Determine the location which male are mostly atttacked.
  • State the average crimes commited in the city.
  • Indicate the region with higest concentration of crimes.
  • specify the most safest location for female.
  • Country - The stste where the crimes occored
  • Agency - The agencies involed in law enforcement.
  • Year - Years in which the incident occured
  • Months - Month of the occurances
  • Index Total - Total numbers of reported cases
  • Violence - The total numbers of violence cases
  • Muder - Cases of muder reported
  • Rape - Rape cases reported
  • Robbery - Total nmber of robbery cases
  • Aggravated Assault - Assult cases
  • Property Total - Total numbers of stolen property
  • Burglary - Burglary cases reported
  • Larceny -Larceny reported
  • Motor Vechicle Theft - Numbers of stolen vechicle
  • Region -Region the incident occred.
